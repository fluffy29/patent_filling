Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need an attorney to file a patent?

+

No, you can file a patent on your own using our platform. However, attorney consultation is recommended for complex inventions or if you want expert review before submission.

How much does attorney consultation cost?

+

30-minute consultations start at $200, while 60-minute sessions are $400. Document reviews are a flat $200 fee.

What is a prior art search?

+

A prior art search identifies existing patents, publications, and products similar to your invention. This helps determine patentability and avoid rejection by USPTO.

How long does document review take?

+

Standard document reviews are completed within 2-3 business days. Expedited reviews (24 hours) are available for an additional fee.

Can I get free legal advice?

+

Yes! You can submit general questions through our "Ask a Question" feature and receive a response within 24 hours at no cost.

Are your attorneys licensed?

+

Yes, all attorneys on our platform are licensed to practice before the USPTO and are members in good standing of their respective state bars.
